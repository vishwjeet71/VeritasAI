The Pipeline

How VeritasAI works

01

Input & Classification

Accepts plain-text claims or article URLs. Uses URL parsing and NLP to route correctly through the pipeline.

02

Claim Extraction

spaCy filters candidate sentences (named entity + verb filter). Groq rephrases and scores each for checkability. Top 5 returned.

03

Source Discovery

Google Fact Check DB queried first. Falls through to GNews search with credibility-scored domain filtering if needed.

04

Evidence Extraction

Sources fetched via trafilatura. Sentences embedded with all-MiniLM-L6-v2. Top 2 most semantically relevant chunks per source selected.

05

LLM Verdict

Claim + evidence sent to Groq (Llama 3). Returns a grounded verdict with explanation and source attribution — not just a summary.
